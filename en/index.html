About me

I am a web developer in training at the Systems for the Internet course at FATEC Rubens Lara, driven by curiosity, creativity and the real desire to transform ideas into digital solutions. I started my journey in Social Media looking for courses in Digital Marketing, ADS and Graphic Design, but soon I discovered my true passion: web development. I like challenges, learning something new every day and creating interfaces that make sense for real people. Today I have the vision that companies and small entrepreneurs need a strong and effective digital presence to grow in today's competitive market. I am here to help transform these needs into reality, creating websites and web applications that are not only visually appealing, but also functional and easy to use.
